Krka Waterfalls
Krka Waterfalls
  • Duration: 09:00h - 17:00h
  • Price per person: 450kn (60€)
  • Price includes: transfer, entrance fees for sights, english speaking guide, Short walking tour in Šibenik and Trogir, Possibility of half daychoosing duration of tour / half day or day trip, Insurance
  • Itinerary:
    09:00: departure from Sun and Sea T.A.
    10:00: arrival to Krka Waterfalls
    14:30: departure from Skradin
    15:00: short stop and walking tour in Šibenik and Trogir
    17:00: arrival to Split
Krka waterfalls are a beautiful and unique national park in Dalmatia. It is unique for it's amazing travertine cascades and waterfalls Skradinski buk, Roški slap and island of Visovac. You will have opportunity to learn more about this amazing national park from local tour guide and later on discover this pearl of Dalmatia by yourself – by walking on walking trails or swimming in front of Skradinski buk. Skradinski buk is one of most beautiful waterfalls in Krka National Park and this area is prefect for enjoying nature.

In addition to that, you will have the opportunity to see historical towns Trogir and Šibenik on our short walking tour on the way back.

Monday - Saturday: 11:00 - 19:00 h
