Rafting
Rafting
  • Duration: 4:00h
  • Schedule: Every day at 8:00 and 14:00
  • Price per person: 300kn (40€)
  • Price includes: Boat, helmet, swim west, paddle, rafting guide, transfer from Split to Omiš by van
  • Itinerary:
    8:00 Meeting point Split harbour office
    8:30 Arrival to Omiš, preparation, safety instructions
    9:00 Start of adventure
    12:00 Return to Omiš
    12:30 Arrival to Split
Located in the heart of middle Dalmatia, historic town of Omiš is best known for 3 things – pirates, beaches and Cetina river. Just 20 km from Split, 30 minutes ride, you can experience the spirit of past ­ climbing to fort Mirabela or for those more adventurous, Fortica fortress where you will be breath taken by the view of surrounding islands and landscape. Want to feel like pirate yourself? Try river Cetina rafting! Cetina river with source in north­western slops of Dinara flows 101 km to the Adriatic Sea. Thanks to thousands years of flowing, Cetina managed to create mystical shapes full of waterfalls, lakes, high cliffs and subterranean tunnels, giving you the ultimate rafting experience. Without a doubt river Cetina is one of the most beautiful places in Croatia where you can experience the magic of a rafting adventure. Located in the spectacular area named after the river, calmly pulls through its canyons and trough countless towns and hills that are slowly rising above it.
