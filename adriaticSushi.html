A place not to be missed when visiting Split, this seasonally open restaurant is a wonderful blend of Mediterranean authenticity and Japan’s famous raw fish specialty. What makes this place so special is the freshness of its sushi, its wonderful presentation, and the professionalism of our staff. Let yourself be mesmerised by our Adriatic tuna dishes, as well as other fresh fish, and see for yourself why Adriatic oysters are the world’s best.
