Quad adventure
Quad adventure
  • Starts: 10:00h (2h) or 18:00h (2h)
  • Price per person: 350kn (47€)
  • Itinerary:
    1. Start at village Žedno.
    2. Instructors give you basic tutorial how to drive Quad or Buggy.
    3. Dirt road driving past village Slatine
    4. Stopping by few view points to enjoy the view of archipelago
    5. End of island Ćiovo we stop on view point from you can enjoy view on city Split, Marjan, Kaštela, island Brač and Šolta. After small break we head back from where we started.

Qualified safety instructor will introduce you to the safe and fun way of exploring our magnificent wilderness upon your very own Quad or Buggy. Riding through the unique Dalmatian bush land and mountain trails is an exciting way to experience the beautiful scenery that the Dalmatian coast has to offer.

Quad adventures offers safe and exciting family adventure in a natural environment. No experience is necessary and full training is provided. We arrange your family adventure to cater for your ability.
