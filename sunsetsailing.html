Sunset Sailing
Sunset Sailing
  • Duration: 18:30h - 20:30h
  • Price per person: 230kn (30€)
  • Price includes:Included Transport by boat Polaris, skipper, snacks with ordered drinks.
  • Price does not include:Drinks
  • Itinerary:
    18:30 Departure from port of Split, cruising into the sunset
    20:30 Return to port of Split
Sunset Cruise is a unique experience that you shouldn’t miss. We all know that, during the day, Croatia’s islands are one of the most beautiful places in the world to spend your holidays. Knowing that, just imagine how much beauty is about to be uncovered when you set sails into the sunset. Wide range of warm, cheerful colours would be released onto the surrounding nature that would result with a feeling of complete and utter joy. Well, that’s just how we feel after each sunset cruise. Listening to sweet music, sipping a drink and listening to stories about the old times, is the original experience you should not miss.
