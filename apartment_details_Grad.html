About this apartment.

Apartment building is in alleyway which makes it quiet during the summer season. Diocletian's palace is 500m away from apartment building and the most attractive beach in Split is 10 minute walk.

There is one bedroom, with two beds which can be made into double bed and TV and few large closets.

Also, there is a sofa in the living room that can sleep one, or can be made into a double bed. There is also LCD TV (HDMI and USB port) with cable and stereo. Free WI-FI is available in the whole apartment.

Apartment is on the third floor of an apartment building. It's location in city center makes it close to markets, bars, coffe-shops, restaurants and every important event during summer.

Bed sheets and towels are at guests' disposal and are changed once a week, or more frequently upon request.
