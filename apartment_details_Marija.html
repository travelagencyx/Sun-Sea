About this apartment.

Apartment Maria is located in the heart of the old town center, and newly built in the first floor. Emperor Diocletian's Palace is only 3 minutes away.

Apartment is situated near City Theater, legendary stadium of local football club Hajduk and main pedestrian street (Marmontova) where you can find a lot of boutiques, main events and traditional fish market. Ferry (that connects all the islands), bus station, and train station are just few minutes away.

All the bars and restaurants are within walking distance so you can relax in Split without a car. The beaches are 10 to 15 minutes away (by walk!). Welcome and enjoy your stay in Split!
