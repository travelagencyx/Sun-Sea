About us

We are small tourist agency from Split. Our main goal is creating welcoming atmosphere and organisational help for guests that decide to visit city of Split. Office is located in city center, 50m from Diocletian's north palace entrance. For our guests, we offer accommodation, tours, car rentals, transportation and restaurant recommendations.
