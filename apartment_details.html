About this apartment.

New apartment located 200m from stadium Poljud and 10 minute walk from Diocletian's palace. Place is in apartment building on 3rd floor and has open view from apartment terrace. Apartment is sunny and airy. Neighborhood is quiet during summer season.

There is one bedroom, with double bed and TV and large closet. Bedroom is directly linked to balcony through doors. Also, there is a sofa in the living room that can sleep one, or can be made into a double bed. There is also LCD TV (HDMI and USB port) with cable and stereo. Balcony combined with a comfy chair offers perfect place for morning coffe/afternoon glass of wine.

Free WI-FI is available in the whole apartment. There is a free parking lot in front of the apartment building. Apartment is on the third floor of an apartment building, there is an elevator. There are couple of markets around the building, as well as bars, coffe-shops and restaurants.

Bed sheets and towels are at guests' disposal and are changed once a week, or more frequently upon request.
